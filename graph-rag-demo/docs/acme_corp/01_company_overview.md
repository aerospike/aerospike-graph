# Acme Corp - Company Overview

**Founded:** 2019 by Sarah Chen and Marcus Williams

**Headquarters:** Austin, Texas

**Mission:** Revolutionizing enterprise data management through innovative graph technology.

## Leadership Team

- **CEO:** Sarah Chen - Co-founder, previously VP Engineering at DataFlow Inc.
- **CTO:** Marcus Williams - Co-founder, PhD in distributed systems from MIT
- **VP Engineering:** James Rodriguez - Joined 2021, leads Platform and Infrastructure teams
- **VP Product:** Lisa Park - Joined 2022, owns product roadmap for NexusDB and GraphSync
- **Head of Security:** David Kim - Joined 2020, responsible for SOC2 compliance and security policies

## Core Products

1. **NexusDB** - Distributed graph database (launched 2020)
2. **GraphSync** - Real-time data synchronization service (launched 2022)
3. **QueryStudio** - Visual query builder and analytics (launched 2023)

## Engineering Teams

- **Platform Team** - Led by Emily Watson, maintains NexusDB core
- **Infrastructure Team** - Led by Alex Chen, handles cloud deployments and SRE
- **Product Team** - Led by Michael Brown, develops GraphSync and QueryStudio
- **Security Team** - Led by David Kim, handles security reviews and compliance

## Key Customers

- TechFlow Systems (Enterprise license since 2021)
- GlobalBank Corp (Financial services deployment)
- HealthData Inc (Healthcare data platform)
